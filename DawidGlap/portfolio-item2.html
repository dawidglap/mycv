Metodo Bussola

Metodo Bussola is a life coaching site, created by my partner and developed by me, with the aim of promoting her coaching activity which she started doing remotely since the beginning of the pandemic.

Main Features:

The ability to book an appointment directly with "Calendly".

Direct contact to whatsapp messaging via the dedicated button.

A form integrated with Javascript that securely receives emails, specifying the information entered by the user in the body.

Integrated Paypal payment service for single or multiple sessions.
