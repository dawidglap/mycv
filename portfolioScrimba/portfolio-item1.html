Here's how Presto works:

Both private users and companies can place advertisements on this online portal. Ads can be of any type, from the sale of objects or services to job advertisements.

How to sell:

Placing an ad on Presto is very simple thanks to an intuitive and not very complex procedure to use. The first thing to do is to access the homepage of the site and click on the 'Insert ad' button. If the user is already registered, he can check all his previous announcements and insert new ones. If, on the other hand, the user is not registered, he can choose to register or post an ad without registration.

The second step will be to enter the category. Once selected, you will need to choose an image of the product you want to sell and provide a title for the ad. If you want to buy the procedure will be even easier. Just click on the "search" button for the object or ad you are looking for and it will appear in a few seconds.
